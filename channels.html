CHANNELS



Person-to-Person


Person-to-person communication is emotionally engaging, persuasive, and effective. It allows for dialogue, participation, and the opportunity to establish trust. Nonverbal cues allow communicators to interpret people's reactions, learn about people's needs, discover what's most meaningful, and adapt their message accordingly.

CHANNELS



Social Media


Social media supports interactive communication and social transparency. Access, meaningful stories, and feedback can generate attention, interest, and broader awareness with large audiences. Communicators can also learn about identities and values, people's frames of reference, local issues, stories, solutions, and goals.

CHANNELS



Traditional Media Channels


Choosing the most appropriate channels for an audience can help you reach, motivate, and engage people in ways that are meaningful for them. Integrate messages across multiple channels, use communications tactics that fit people's needs, and work with trusted messengers to earn an audiences' trust.

CHANNELS



Trusted Messengers


People trust messengers who are familiar and credible. Connect with your audience to discover whose advice they share and who they look to for insight. Use variety of trusted messengers to connect broadly, and share simple, repeated messages.
